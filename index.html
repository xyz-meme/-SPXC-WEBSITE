📚 Technical Terms

  • 📈 Stock – A share in the ownership of a company.
  • 🐂 Bull Market – When prices go up.
  • 🐻 Bear Market – When prices go down.
  • 🚀 Moon – When a token skyrockets in price.
  • 💥 Dump – Sudden decrease in value.
  • 📉 Limit Order – An order to buy/sell at a specific price.
  • 🪙 Market Order – An order to buy/sell immediately at market price.
  • 💸 Dividend – Company profits paid to shareholders.
  • 📊 IPO – Initial Public Offering, first sale of stock.
  • 🧠 DYOR – Do Your Own Research.
  • 💰 Share – One unit of stock ownership.
  • 👀 Volume – Amount traded.
  • 🛑 Stop Loss – Limit your losses by auto-selling.
  • 📈 RSI – Relative Strength Index, technical indicator.
  • 🔄 EMA – Exponential Moving Average.
  • 💼 Portfolio – Your investment collection.
  • 👔 Broker – Middleman who buys/sells stock for you.
  • 🧮 P/E Ratio – Price to earnings ratio.
  • 🧊 Liquidity – Ease of converting to cash.
  • ⚖️ Market Cap – Total value of all coins.
